My story

I grew up surrounded by family in computer science, so I was exposed to it young — and, honestly, I resisted it at first. It felt assigned to me rather than chosen, and the syntax never clicked.

High school flipped that completely. Joining CS clubs and learning from an inspiring CS teacher showed me the genuinely cool side of it. For the first time I chose computer science for myself — and then leaned all the way in, entering competitions to grow my skills and my curiosity.
